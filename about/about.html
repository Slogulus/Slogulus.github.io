How to Use This Tool

This AI Research Helper is designed specifically for trades professionals to understand complex research papers and technical concepts using familiar analogies and practical examples.

Getting Started

  1. Select Your Trade: Choose your profession from the trade selector panel (Electrician, Mechanic, Plumber, etc.)
  2. Load a Paper (Optional): Enter an arXiv or Archive.org URL for paper-specific questions
  3. Start Chatting: Ask questions and get explanations tailored to your trade background

Using the Chat Interface

Without a Paper Loaded

Ask general technical questions and get trade-specific explanations:

  • "Explain quantum computing"
  • "What is blockchain technology?"
  • "How does artificial intelligence work?"
  • "What is cloud computing?"

With a Paper Loaded

The AI has access to the paper's content and explains it using your trade background:

  • Ask about the research: "What is this paper about?"
  • Request trade-specific summaries: "Explain this like I'm a [your trade]"
  • Get practical applications: "How could this technology be used in my field?"
  • Understand complex concepts: "Break down this technical concept for me"

Tips for Best Results

  • Select Your Trade: Always choose your profession first for the most relevant explanations
  • Ask Practical Questions: "How does this apply to my work?" or "What's the real-world use?"
  • Request Analogies: "Explain this using [your trade] examples"
  • Be Specific: Ask about particular aspects you want to understand
  • Use Clear Paper: Click "Clear" between different papers to avoid confusion
